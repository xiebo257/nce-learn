# NCE3 Lesson 40 - Who's Who

## EN: 1. It has never been explained why university students seem to enjoy practical jokes more than anyone else.
美音发音: /ɪt hæz ˈnɛvɚ bɪn ɪkˈspleɪnd waɪ ˌjunəˈvɝsəti ˈstudənts sim tə‿ɪnˈdʒɔɪ ˈpræktɪkəl dʒoʊks mɔr ðən ˈɛniwʌn ɛls/

结构: It 作形式主语；why 引导真正的主语从句；seem to do 表“似乎做”。

时态: 现在完成时被动 `has never been explained`；从句一般现在时。

中: 大学生为什么似乎比任何人都更喜欢恶作剧，这一点从来没有得到解释。

词: explain 解释； university student 大学生； practical joke 恶作剧； more than anyone else 比任何其他人都更；

## EN: 5. When a student recently saw two workmen using a pneumatic drill outside his university, he immediately telephoned the police and informed them that two students dressed up as workmen were tearing up the road with a pneumatic drill.

## EN: 20. Only when he saw that the man was actually telephoning the police did he realize that they had all been the victims of a hoax.
美音发音: /ˈoʊnli wɛn hi sɔ ðæt ðə mæn wəz ˈæktʃuəli ˈtɛləˌfoʊnɪŋ ðə pəˈlis dɪd hi ˈriəˌlaɪz ðæt ðeɪ hæd ɔl bɪn ðə ˈvɪktəmz‿əv‿ə hoʊks/

## 词汇总结
恶作剧与骗局: practical joke 恶作剧； hoax 骗局； deception 欺骗； play jokes on sb 捉弄某人； victim 受害者；
警察与执法: police 警察； policeman 警察； under arrest 被逮捕； station 警察局； permission 许可； by force 强行；
动作短语: specialize in 专门从事； dress up as 装扮成； tear up 挖开； hang up 挂断电话； take sb seriously 把某人当回事； telephone for help 打电话求助； lose one's temper 发脾气；

## 句型总结
`It has never been explained why...`: It 作形式主语，why 从句是真正主语。
`which no self-respecting student would ever indulge in`: which 引导定语从句，介词 in 留在句尾。
`be to do`: `they were not to take him seriously` 表安排、命令或提醒。
`Only when... did he realize...`: only + 状语置首，主句部分倒装。
`before being taken...`: 介词后用动名词，被动形式为 being done。

## 时态总结
一般过去时是故事叙述主线：`saw`, `telephoned`, `informed`, `went`, `told`, `arrived`, `asked`。
过去完成时表示更早动作：`had hung up`, `had dressed up`, `had all been`。
过去进行时表现当时正在发生的动作：`were tearing up`, `was playing`, `was telephoning`。
现在完成时被动用于开头议论：`has never been explained`。

## 情态/语气总结
`would ever indulge in` 表假设评价，强调“有自尊的学生绝不会”。
`could watch and hear` 表过去能力。
`might make a telephone call` 是间接引语中的委婉请求。
`would hardly be necessary` 带讽刺语气，意思是“恐怕没有必要”。

## 本课连读注意点
辅音 + 元音连读: `specialize in` -> /ˈspɛʃəˌlaɪz‿ɪn/；`type of` -> /taɪp‿əv/；`put out a` -> /pʊt‿aʊt‿ə/；`dressed up as` -> /drɛst‿ʌp‿æz/；`pointed out` -> /ˈpɔɪntɪd‿aʊt/。
弱读词: `a`, `of`, `to`, `and`, `that` 要轻读并粘住前后，如 `piece of advance information` -> /pis‿əv ədˈvæns ˌɪnfɚˈmeɪʃən/。
意群停顿: 长句第 5 句可按 `When a student recently saw... / he immediately telephoned... / and informed them that...` 分组。第 20 句按 `Only when he saw... / did he realize...` 分组。
