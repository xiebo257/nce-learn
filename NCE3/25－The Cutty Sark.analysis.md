# NCE3 Lesson 25 - The Cutty Sark

## EN: 1. One of the most famous sailing ships of the nineteenth century, the Cutty Sark, can still be seen at Greenwich.
美音发音: /wʌn əv ðə moʊst ˈfeɪməs ˈseɪlɪŋ ʃɪps əv ðə ˌnaɪnˈtinθ ˈsɛntʃəri ðə ˈkʌti sɑrk kæn stɪl bi sin æt ˈɡrɛnɪtʃ/
结构: the Cutty Sark 为同位语；can be seen 为情态被动。
时态: 一般现在时；情态被动。
中: 19 世纪最著名的帆船之一“卡蒂萨克”号，如今仍能在格林尼治看到。
词: sailing ship 帆船； century 世纪； Greenwich 格林尼治；

## EN: 2. She stands on dry land and is visited by thousands of people each year.
美音发音: /ʃi stændz ɑn draɪ lænd ənd ɪz ˈvɪzɪɾɪd baɪ ˈθaʊzəndz əv ˈpipəl itʃ jɪr/
结构: stands 和 is visited 为并列谓语；第二个为被动。
时态: 一般现在时；一般现在时被动。
中: 她停放在陆地上，每年有成千上万人参观。
词: dry land 陆地； thousands of 成千上万；

## EN: 3. She serves as an impressive reminder of the great ships of the past.
美音发音: /ʃi sɝvz æz ən ɪmˈprɛsɪv rɪˈmaɪndɚ əv ðə ɡreɪt ʃɪps əv ðə pæst/
结构: serve as 表“作为”；of the past 作后置定语。
时态: 一般现在时。
中: 她令人印象深刻地提醒人们记起过去的伟大船只。
词: serve as 作为； impressive 令人印象深刻的； reminder 提醒物；

## EN: 4. Before they were replaced by steamships, sailing vessels like the Cutty Sark were used to carry tea from China and wool from Australia.
美音发音: /bɪˈfɔr ðeɪ wɚ rɪˈpleɪst baɪ ˈstimˌʃɪps ˈseɪlɪŋ ˈvɛsəlz laɪk ðə ˈkʌti sɑrk wɚ juzd tə ˈkæri ti frəm ˈtʃaɪnə ənd wʊl frəm ɔˈstreɪljə/
结构: Before 引导时间状语从句；主句为被动结构 `were used to carry`。
时态: 一般过去时被动。
中: 在被蒸汽船取代之前，像“卡蒂萨克”这样的帆船曾被用来从中国运茶、从澳大利亚运羊毛。
词: replace 取代； steamship 蒸汽船； vessel 船只； wool 羊毛；

## EN: 6. The only other ship to match her was the Thermopylae.

## EN: 7. Both these ships set out from Shanghai on June 18th, 1872 on an exciting race to England.
美音发音: /boʊθ ðiz ʃɪps sɛt aʊt frəm ʃæŋˈhaɪ ɑn dʒun eɪˈtinθ ˌeɪˈtin ˈsɛvənti tu ɑn ən ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ reɪs tə ˈɪŋɡlənd/

## EN: 8. This race, which went on for exactly four months, was the last of its kind.

## EN: 9. It marked the end of the great tradition of ships with sails and the beginning of a new era.

## EN: 10. The first of the two ships to reach Java after the race had begun was the Thermopylae, but on the Indian Ocean, the Cutty Sark took the lead.

中: 比赛开始后，最先到达爪哇的是“塞莫皮莱”号，但在印度洋上，“卡蒂萨克”号领先了。

## EN: 11. It seemed certain that she would be the first ship home, but during the race she had a lot of bad luck.

## 词汇总结
船只与航行: sailing ship 帆船； steamship 蒸汽船； vessel 船只； rudder 舵； plank 木板； port 港口； Equator 赤道；
比赛与速度: race 比赛； take the lead 领先； lose one's lead 失去领先； tremendous speed 惊人速度； delay 延误；
动作短语: serve as 作为； be used to carry 被用来运输； set out 出发； be struck by 遭遇； have sth fitted 安装某物；

## 句型总结
`one of the fastest...that has ever been built`: 最高级 + 定语从句。
`during which...`: 介词 + which 引导定语从句。
`There was a danger that...`: that 引导同位语从句。
`It was impossible for...to...`: it 形式主语。
`if she had not..., she would have...`: 与过去事实相反的虚拟条件句。

## 时态总结
一般现在时用于介绍保存现状：`can still be seen`, `stands`, `serves`。
一般过去时用于比赛叙事：`set out`, `marked`, `took`, `lost`, `arrived`。
被动语态突出船只遭遇和修理：`was struck`, `was torn away`, `was made`, `was fitted`。
过去完成时和虚拟语气用于假设结果：`had not lost`, `would have won`。

## 情态/语气总结
`can still be seen` 表现在仍能被看到。
`would be torn away` 表当时担心的可能结果。
`would have won` 表对过去未发生结果的肯定推断。
文章语气对“卡蒂萨克”号虽败犹荣。

## 本课连读注意点
辅音 + 元音连读: `set out` -> /sɛt‿aʊt/；`fitted with`、`called in at`、`lost her` 注意弱读衔接。
弱读词: `of`, `to`, `and`, `that`, `as` 要轻读，如 `the beginning of a new era`。
意群停顿: 第 15 句按 `This greatly reduced... / for there was a danger / that if... / this rudder...` 分组。
