词汇总结

巧合与小说: credulous 轻信的； coincidence 巧合； improbable 不大可能的； naive 幼稚的； incredible 难以置信的； circumstance 情况；

亲属与身份: long-lost brother 失散多年的兄弟； maidservant 女仆； resemblance 相似； be presumed dead 被认为已死； reunite 重逢；

战争经历: be killed in action 阵亡； be wounded 受伤； be separated from 与……分离； unit 部队； air raid 空袭； record 记录；

动作短语: bring about 导致； pour scorn on 嘲笑/嗤之以鼻； be acquainted with 熟悉； make one's way back 返回； settle down 定居；

句型总结

less...than we used to be: 与过去状态比较。

would bring...: would 表过去习惯。

anyone but...would find...: 除了……以外任何人都会觉得……。

be thought to have been killed: 被认为已经遇害，不定式完成被动。

Assuming that...: 分词短语作假设/原因状语。

时态总结

一般现在时用于开头议论现代读者：are less credulous, conspire。

一般过去时用于祖父讲述的故事：told, found, stopped, commented, sent。

过去完成时用于汉斯先前遭遇：had been killed, had gone on, had been sent, had been bombed, had remained。

过去完成时被动很多，突出战争中身份记录被动消失：had been destroyed, had been killed。

might be his brother 和 might be right 表布斯曼太太谨慎的可能性判断。

was thought to have been killed 表外界误判。

标题 A Chance in a Million 强调极小概率的真实巧合。

本课连读注意点

辅音 + 元音连读: bring about -> /brɪŋ‿əˈbaʊt/；poured scorn on -> /pɔrd skɔrn‿ɑn/；pointing out -> /ˈpɔɪntɪŋ‿aʊt/；gone on -> /ɡɔn‿ɑn/。

弱读词: to, of, and, that, as 多弱读，如 a series of coincidences。

意群停顿: 第 20 句按 Assuming that... / Hans settled down... / where he had remained... 分组。
